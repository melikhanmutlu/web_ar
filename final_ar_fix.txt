Solution:
- Mobile (≤640px): Show NATIVE AR button, hide custom button

- Native AR button works perfectly on mobile devices
- Custom button shows QR modal on desktop

Changes:
- Remove global hide of native AR button

- Handles iOS Quick Look automatically
- Handles Android Scene Viewer automatically
- No JavaScript needed - pure native functionality
- Custom button only for desktop QR modal
