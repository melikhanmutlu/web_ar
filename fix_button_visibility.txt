Fix AR button visibility on mobile by adding z-index

Problem:
- AR button not visible on mobile devices
- Model-viewer was covering the buttons
- Missing z-index on bottom-right-controls

Solution:
- Add z-index: 2000 to bottom-right-controls
- Ensure buttons stay above model-viewer
- Apply to both desktop and mobile

Result:
- AR button now visible on mobile
- All control buttons properly layered
- No more hidden buttons
